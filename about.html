FYI Lab

About

A one-person practice focused on making complex information legible.

What I do

FYI Lab helps teams turn data into decisions by improving how data is explored and communicated. The work is usually a mix of exploratory analysis, visualization, and editorial structure.

How I work

I keep the process lightweight: align on the question, prototype quickly, then refine hierarchy and annotation so the visuals can be read without a meeting.

Typical deliverables

  • Interactive data stories
  • Dashboards and chart systems
  • Exploratory analysis + reproducible notebooks
  • Chart reviews and refactors
